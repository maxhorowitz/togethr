ABOUT US

TOGETHR is an iOS application that enables you and your friends to leverage your buying power as customers in order to receive major discounts on premium cannabis products – removing the cost and inconvenience that comes with ordering from an expensive dispensary. TOGETHR ensures that you only see products relevant to your networks, tastes, and interests meaning less time searching and more time seshing!

OUR TEAM

Calvin Hulse is a senior at Cornell University studying Entrepreneurial Strategy and Creative Innovation. He has previously interned with McDonald’s, ALICE, NSG/SWAT, and is the former Creative Director of Authentic Social, all of which have given him valuable insight into growth strategy and relevant industry dynamics. Calvin has a passion for creating novel products and business models which challenge convention, and is extremely excited to apply his strategic vision to TOGETHR. Originally from Onalaska, WI, Calvin plans to reside in Manhattan, NY upon graduation.
